// Banijay · 2022 to 2023

Audience and Content Performance Analysis

Data science to enhance media strategy

An analysis of content and ratings data for Banijay, aimed at improving content strategy and audience engagement.

My roleData analyst
DurationNov 2022 to Jan 2023
ClientBanijay
FocusData analysis
01 / overview

Working with Banijay, I analysed content and ratings data to support their content strategy. I cleaned and linked the data with a lookup table, then studied audience preferences, top-performing hosts, and the best shows and fragments.

02 / analysis
  • Ratings analysed over time, with a look at the correlation between viewership and social media trends such as Twitter.
  • Visualisations built to communicate findings clearly to the Banijay team.
  • A predictive model for ratings that, while not successful, produced useful insight and lessons.
03 / impact

The work gave Banijay a clearer picture of audience preferences and content performance to support their decisions.
