AI-Powered Spider Detector

Convolutional neural networks for tourist safety

An AI-powered app concept to improve safety for tourists in Australia by identifying spiders and assessing their threat level, built around a CNN with explainability and a user-centred prototype.

01 / overview

The goal was an app that could reliably identify spiders and flag their threat level to help reduce bites during exploration. I built an image-recognition model with deep learning and verified its behaviour with explainability tools.

02 / highlights
  • A CNN reaching 89% accuracy on the test set.
  • Grad-CAM used to visualise and confirm that the model focused on the right features.
  • A user-centred prototype in Proto.io with colour-coded threat indicators, refined with early user feedback.
