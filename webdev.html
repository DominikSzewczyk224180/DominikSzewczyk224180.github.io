01 / what I build for you

You do not need to know anything technical. Tell me about your business and I take care of the rest, from the first sketch to a site that is live on Google and easy for you to run.

design + build

A site made for you

I build your website from scratch in clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, shaped around what your business actually needs. No bulky page builders, just a fast, tidy site.

online

Live on your domain

I sort out hosting and connect everything to your own address, so the site is online, secure, and ready for visitors without you touching a server.

google

Found on Google

I set the site up for Google search and Google Maps, with the right titles, descriptions, and structure, so local customers can actually find you.

admin panel

Update it yourself

A friendly admin panel where you add news, photos, prices, or offers on your own. No code, and no monthly agency fees just to change a phone number.

newsletter

Stay in touch

An optional newsletter and contact forms, so you can collect enquiries and reach your customers directly from your own site.

payments

Take payments online

When you want to sell or take bookings, I can connect online payments and order forms straight into the site.
