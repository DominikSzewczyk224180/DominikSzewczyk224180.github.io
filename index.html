01 / about

I am Dominik, a Data Science and AI graduate from Breda University of Applied Sciences, where I finished with cum laude distinction. I like the messy part of the job: taking raw, real-world data and turning it into something a business can use, whether that is a forecast, a dashboard, or a model running in production.

Across my degree I worked on real projects with companies and research institutions in the Netherlands, from emotion analysis in video and 3D digital twins to logistics prediction and a physics-informed model for plasma reactor optimisation. I care about the whole chain, from raw data to a tool the end user can actually operate.
