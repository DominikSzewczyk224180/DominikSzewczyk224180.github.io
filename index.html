01 / about

I am Dominik, a Data Science and AI graduate based in Breda, the Netherlands. I enjoy turning raw data into something a business can actually use: a clear forecast, a dashboard people open every day, or a model running live in production.

During my studies I worked on real projects with companies and research groups across the Netherlands, from emotion analysis in video and 3D digital twins to logistics forecasting and a physics-informed model for plasma reactors. I like owning the whole process, from the first look at the data all the way to a finished tool someone can use on their own.
